Nefertiti

Nefertiti, or Neffie, as we call her, appears to be an all-grey cat, but she is really a grey-on-grey tabby. She lost her stripes she had as a kitten. She is from the same litter as Rufus. She will cut a bitch for crab.

Favorite things

  • People food
  • Small birds
  • Not her brother Rufus
